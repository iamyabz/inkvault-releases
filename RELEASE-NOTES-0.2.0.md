# InkVault 0.2.0 (Beta)

The first milestone beta — everything InkVault does, in one build. macOS · Apple Silicon.

## Search & Ask
- **Search across everything** — handwriting, PDFs & scans, audio, video, spreadsheets, and Word/PowerPoint/Keynote — as if it were all typed.
- **Ask AI with citations** — ask one document or **⌘-select several and chat across them**. Every answer links back to the exact page, second, or moment.

## Watch & Listen
- **Native video player** with a synced, click-to-seek transcript, auto chapters, and **1×–3× playback**.
- **Chat a recording** — ask a question and the answer’s timestamps jump the player right to the moment.
- Audio notes get the same speed control and transcript search.

## Import
- **Word, PowerPoint & Keynote** render faithfully (not just a text dump).
- Handwriting & scans become fully searchable PDFs; spreadsheets become answerable in plain English.

## Private by default
- Your library, transcripts, and search index stay on your Mac. Bring your own AI key — only what’s needed for a question is ever sent.

## Housekeeping
- One-click **auto-update** keeps this beta current.
- Requires macOS 14+ on Apple Silicon.

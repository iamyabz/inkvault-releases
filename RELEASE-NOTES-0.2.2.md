# InkVault 0.2.2 (Beta)

Bring your own AI, plus clearer settings. macOS · Apple Silicon.

## New
- **Answer with any model.** Ask AI and summaries can now run on **OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, or OpenRouter** — not just Gemini. Pick your provider in **Settings ▸ AI Model ▸ Answer model** and paste your key.
- **Clearer keys.** Settings now spells out what each key does: **Gemini** (required — reads your notes & powers search), an optional **answer-model** key, and optional **Cloud Vision** for printed pages.

## Under the hood
- Security hardening for licensing and the auto-updater (verified, tamper-checked).
- Subscription-ready licenses — nothing changes for current beta testers.

Search, OCR, and indexing still run on Gemini, so a Gemini key stays required — this just lets you choose the model that writes your answers.

Updates install in one click from **InkVault → Check for Updates…** (or automatically).
